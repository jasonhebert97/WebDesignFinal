EXERCISES

> 7 Principles of Typographic Contrast

Stylized version of the Seven Principles of Typographic Contrast written by John D. Berry. The goal was to increase familiarity and confidence with basic HTML and CSS.

> Josef Müller Brockman Poster

Recreation of a Joseph Müller-Brockman poster done with only HTML and CSS. The focus was to become familiar with positioning, manipulating, and animating elements of a webpage.
