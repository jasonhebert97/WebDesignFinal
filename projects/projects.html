> climate clicker

PROMPTChoose a span of time to represent as a multi-page website.

This project utilizes data extracted from NASA's Climate Vital Signs page. Carbon dioxide, global temperature, ice sheets, and sea level data points are visualized for each year. By dragging icons to the designated area and moving the slider at the bottom, the user is able to see the different changes that have occured wihtin the past years.
